About Me

A professional, future-focused learner shaping a path through research, engineering, teaching, and advanced intelligent technologies.

I am Md Borhan Uddin Shuvo, currently studying Computer Science and Engineering at United International University, Bangladesh. I am deeply interested in the future of computing and the possibilities created by intelligent systems.

My academic and personal journey is centered around AI, ML, LLM research, robotics, semiconductor-oriented technological thinking, and agentic AI.
